# Governance

tuiweave-agent-kit is currently maintained by Ishan Sain, who has final authority
over scope, workflow design, releases, security response, and repository
administration. Material decisions are recorded in repository documentation,
issues, or pull requests.

Contributors who demonstrate sustained, constructive participation may be
invited to triage or maintain relevant areas. Access is granted and revoked by
the maintainer according to project needs and trust.

If active maintenance ends, the maintainer will seek a responsible successor or
archive the project with its status clearly documented. There is no guaranteed
succession or support period.
